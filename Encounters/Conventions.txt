	Conventions
	--------------
	Alert variables:
		Warnings: <name>warn
		Durations: <name>dur
		Cooldowns: <name>cd

	Combat log arguments
	--------------
	1 - srcGUID
	2 - srcName
	3 - srcFlags
	4 - dstGUID
	5 - dstName
	6 - dstFlags
	7 - spellID
	8 - spellName

	functions: &<func>&
	set = "<INCR or DECR>|<number>"
